Myself, and 4 other people made up our team for the 2021 global game jam. Our team name was the 8-bit Knights. I created a small knight logo that I then scattered around different rooms of our game (see if you can find it below!).
The game concept was simple; you play as a janitor in a school who is working night-shift. The janitor, remembering his wife who was murdered in the school, goes to work only to find his wife’s shoe in one of the halls. With signs of a break-in, you have to investigate the rest of the school to find out what happened.

My role was to create the art assets within the game environment and build out the level. Taking inspiration from games such as Darkwood, Little Nightmares and Silent Hill series, I went for a decrepit style. Keeping to the horror theme, I used dull colour schemes to maintain a dark atmosphere. For the map design, I took direct inspiration from the game Silent Hill 4 - in which the main character ‘draws’ his own map in his journal. To re-create this effect, I drew out the map freehand using a graphics tablet.
Although this was my first game jam, I had a lot of fun working with the rest of the team and creating a game together. I learned a lot in a short period of time, from learning more about level design to getting to know Game Maker. I will definitely be aiming to work on other game jams in the future.
